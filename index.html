Blueprint: 24/7 AI Ghostwriting

Ghostwriting is no longer just for authors. In 2026, founders, VCs, and tech leaders need a constant presence on X, LinkedIn, and Threads. They have the money, but they don't have the time. That's where you come in.

Step 1: The "Invisible" Service

You don't sell "AI writing." You sell "Thought Leadership." Offer to manage 3 posts a day for a client. Use free tiers of Claude or Gemini to study their past voice and generate new insights.

Step 2: The 24/7 Automation

Use tools like Buffer or Hypefury (Free Tiers) to schedule a week of content in 30 minutes. Your "worker" (the AI) works 24/7 while you focus on finding the next client.

Step 3: Finding Clients ($0 Cost)

  • Search LinkedIn for "Founder" + "Recently Funded."
  • Send a personalized DM with 3 "Gift" posts you wrote for them.
  • Close the deal at $500 - $1,000 / month per client.
